About

Selfthy

The purpose of this website is to promote yourself, especially your social medias or anything that you do and would like more people to see or gain more followers!

You can put any social medias (blogs, Instagram, Facebook, Tumblr, Youtube, anything you can think of) that you'd want to expose to more people, into one centralize website *cough*Selfthy*cough*. Here you can list all your social medias accounts, post any ads that relate to those social medias. Copy the URL of your profile and share it anywhere! This way anyone visiting your profile can see all the social medias that you want them to know about! They might visit and follow you!

You could also browse what others are posting on Selfthy, explore their profiles, maybe they have similar interests as you or looking for more followers just like you! Check out their social medias, give them attention, follow them, maybe they'll follow you back!

Login to start editing your profile and post ads! Or Register for an account.

Create Profile. Add Social Medias. Post Ads to lure viewers. Wait. Gain views, followers, or attention I guess.

Just have fun!
